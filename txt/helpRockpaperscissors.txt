**rockpaperscissors**
Play rock paper scissors against other players with this command.

*Required*
__opponent__
Set this to *bot* if you want to compete against the random bot or to *human* if you want to set up a new game against a different player or want to join an ongoing game.
__choice__
Your choice. If you are the first player, other players can not see your selected choice before they select theirs.
