__**Available commands:**__
`[a][b][c]`

[a]
```o : Open Tournanent
c : Cash Tournament```

[b]
```q : Qualifiers
d : Day Final
s : Semifinals
f : Finals```

[c]
```  : Base Tournament
ck: Cities & Knights Tournament```


**Example:**
`os : Semifinals for a regular Open Base Tournament`

*Keep in mind that not all combinations exist!*
