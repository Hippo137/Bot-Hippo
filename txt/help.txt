**Available commands:**
[a][b][c]

*[a]*
o : Open Tourmanent
c : Cash Tournament

*[b]*
q : Qualifiers
d : Day Final
s : Semifinals
f : Finals

*[c]*
  : Base Tournament
ck: Cities & Knights Tournament


**Example:**
os : Semifinals for a regular Open Base Tournament 

*Keep in mind that not all combinations exist!*
