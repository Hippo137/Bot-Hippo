__**Available commands:**__
`[a][b][c]`

[a]
```o : Open Tournanent
c : Cash Tournament```
[b]
```r : Qualifiers
d : Day Final
q : Quarterfinals
s : Semifinals
f : Finals```
[c]
```  : Base Tournament (Fast)
ck: Cities & Knights
vf: Very Fast
sh: Shuffle Board```

**Example:**
`os : Semifinals for a regular Open Base Tournament`

*Keep in mind that not all combinations exist!*
